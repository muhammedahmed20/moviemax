Movie Description

A fresh reimagining of the iconic Marvel hero, The Amazing Spider-Man follows teenager Peter Parker, who uncovers hidden secrets about his parents and stumbles upon a powerful discovery inside Oscorp. After being bitten by a genetically enhanced spider, Peter begins to develop extraordinary abilities that push him into a new life as a masked hero. As he adapts to his powers, he faces the dangerous and unpredictable Lizard, a scientist whose experiment goes terribly wrong. The film blends action, emotion, and high-stakes adventure, highlighting Peter’s struggle between being a normal student and the responsibility that comes with becoming Spider-Man.
